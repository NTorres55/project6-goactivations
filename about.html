All About Go! Activations

Go! Activations officially launched in 2020 with a focus on brand activation events. However, we are no strangers to fun event planning, as we have been holding engaging and memorable events for years under our sister company, Pop! Events Groups, since 2005. We originally worked on brand activation events upon special requests from our clients, and we absolutely loved being involved in these events! We thought brand activation events were so awesome that they deserved to be under a seperate group in order to fully focus our efforts on them, and thus Go! Activations was born. We are looking forward to working with you to upgrade your branding with a memorable event- so Let’s Get Going!
